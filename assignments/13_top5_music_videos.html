Week 13: Assignment #13

Top 5 Music Videos

DUE: Tuesday, 4/18 @ 5pm



  • Create a page with your top 5 favorite music videos embedded as HTML video elements or YouTube iFrame based players within the page.
  • Use jQuery to control the playback of the different videos.
  • Create a drop-down menu to choose which video you would like to control, and create two different buttons to play/pause the selected video.
  • Create a slider to adjust the volume of the selected video.
  • If using the YouTube iFrame to embed videos in your page, you will need to use the YouTube iFrame Player API to adjust the properties of the player through JavaScript.

Upload your completed assignment by the due date specified above and make sure that you add a working link to it on your class page, otherwise it will not be reviewed and you will not receive credit.
