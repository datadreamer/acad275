Stripping and Reformatting Phone Numbers

  • Create a form with a text field that is labeled "phonenumber".
  • When the field's content changes, strip out common phone formatting characters:
    • Parenthesis: ( and ).
    • Dashes: -.
    • Whitespace: " ".
    • Replace them with nothing (empty quotes with no space).
  • Verify that what is left is exactly 10 characters.
  • Update the content of the field with the new string.
  • When the submit button is pressed, make an alert with the reformatted number in (123) 456-7890 formatting.
